1. Platform Overview

Jaiben Mobility is an EV bike rental platform with an integrated battery swap network, built for electric bike riders in Bangladesh. The primary business is EV rental — riders rent bikes from Jaiben's fleet on hourly, daily, or monthly plans, use them for daily commuting or gig delivery work, and earn income while riding. Battery swap is the enabling infrastructure: when a rented bike's battery runs low, the rider visits any Jaiben-authorised partner shop, swaps the depleted pack for a charged one, and continues riding without interruption.

The platform manages the complete rental lifecycle — bike discovery, rental initiation, real-time GPS tracking, automated billing, return and damage assessment — alongside the battery swap flow, JCoin wallet, partner shop network, and FOCO (Franchise Owned, Company Operated) investor management.

5. Battery Theft Prevention

Battery packs are high-value (BDT 25,000–45,000). Four protection layers:

ControlImplementation
Signed QR tokenHMAC-SHA256: pack_id, shop_id, issue_date, server secret. Verified on every scan. Copied/printed QR rejected.
Network bindingUnique pack_id. Unregistered QR → alert + admin log.
Shop assignment lockPack at Shop A cannot be AVAILABLE at Shop B without admin transfer. Cross-shop alert triggered.
Rider bindingIN_USE pack linked to rider_id. Return verifies match. Mismatch → review alert.
Unreturned escalation72h: push. 96h: 2nd push. 120h: blocked, JCoin hold = replacement value. 144h: suspended.
Return incentiveReturn within 24h: 2 JCoin bonus (admin-configurable).

18. Risk Register

Comprehensive risk assessment covering technical, operational, financial, and security risks. Each risk is evaluated on likelihood (Low/Medium/High) and impact (Low/Medium/High/Critical) with specific, actionable mitigation strategies.

18.1 Technical Risks

RiskLikelihoodImpactMitigation
GPS tracker hardware failureMediumHighAlert on no-telemetry > 15 min. Redis fallback for last-known position. Backup: cell tower triangulation (v2). Fleet dashboard highlights non-reporting bikes.
MQTT broker failureLowMediumEMQX cluster (3 nodes) with auto-failover. Redis fallback caches last-known positions. GPS data buffered on device and replayed on reconnection.
BariKoi API downtimeLowMediumAutomatic fallback to Google Maps SDK. Cached map tiles for recently viewed areas. Geocoding queue with retry logic.
Redis Sentinel failover lagLowHigh3-node Sentinel cluster with quorum. Sub-millisecond failover. Swap/rental locks have TTL safety net. Monitoring alerts on sentinel state change.
Database connection exhaustionLowHighPgBouncer with max 100 connections regardless of pod count. Connection recycling. Auto-scale pods do not bypass pooler.
RabbitMQ queue buildupLowMediumDLX for failed messages. Consumer auto-restart. Queue depth monitoring with alert at threshold. Manual purge capability in admin.

RiskLikelihoodImpactMitigation
Rider never returns bikeMediumCriticalDeposit mechanism. GPS tracking. Geofence alerts. Escalation timeline (72h→96h→120h→144h). Police report. Insurance claim. Recovery team dispatch at +48h.
False damage claimsMediumMediumMandatory pre/post photos from rider + staff. GPS-validated timestamps. Side-by-side comparison in admin. 24-hour dispute window. Admin as final arbiter.
Shop collusion (fake swaps)LowMediumAnti-theft QR with HMAC binding. Cross-shop scan alerts. Monthly stock reconciliation. Anomaly detection on swap patterns per shop. Payout block on discrepancy.

Bike returned to wrong locationMediumLowBariKoi geofence around authorised return locations. App only allows "End Rental" when within geofence. Staff confirms location via GPS on return photo.
High condition-report storage costsMediumLowCompress photos before S3 upload (WebP, 80% quality). Auto-archive reports > 6 months to cold storage (Glacier-class). Lifecycle policy deletes after 2 years.

RiskLikelihoodImpactMitigation
Battery pack theftMediumHighHMAC-signed QR with network binding. Shop assignment lock. Unreturned escalation (72h→144h). JCoin hold = replacement value. Physical Jaiben branding reduces resale value.
